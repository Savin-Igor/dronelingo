CAA Latvia administers the qualification process for remote pilots operating in Latvia under Regulation (EU) 2019/947. The process depends on the intended subcategory of operation.

## A1/A3 qualification {#a1-a3}

The A1/A3 online exam is conducted through the e.caa.gov.lv portal:

1. Study the online training module (free, available in Latvian).
2. Pass the online knowledge test (40 questions, 75% pass mark, 30-minute limit).
3. Download the A1/A3 certificate of completion immediately after passing.

The exam may be retaken after a cooling-off period if failed. The certificate is valid indefinitely but must be kept up to date with regulatory changes.

## A2 qualification {#a2}

The A2 certificate requires:

1. A valid A1/A3 certificate.
2. Passing the A2 theoretical knowledge exam at a CAA Latvia examination centre (not online).
3. Completing self-declared practical self-study training (5 hours minimum recommended).
4. Applying for the A2 certificate through the e.caa.gov.lv portal.

## Official source

Current exam dates, locations, and fees are listed on the CAA Latvia website.
