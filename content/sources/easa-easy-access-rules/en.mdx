The EASA Easy Access Rules for UAS is a consolidated, freely accessible document that combines Regulations (EU) 2019/947 and 2019/945 with the accompanying Acceptable Means of Compliance (AMC) and Guidance Material (GM). It is the most practical single reference for understanding how EU drone law applies in practice.

## What it contains

- Full text of Regulation (EU) 2019/947 (operations rules)
- Full text of Regulation (EU) 2019/945 (technical requirements)
- AMC and GM for each article — explanatory notes that clarify how to apply the rules
- Cross-references between provisions
- Latest consolidated version includes amendments through 2024

## Acceptable Means of Compliance (AMC)

AMC documents describe methods that, when followed, are accepted by the competent authority as demonstrating compliance with the regulation. They are not mandatory — operators may use alternative means, but must demonstrate equivalent compliance.

## Guidance Material (GM)

GM provides non-binding explanations and examples to aid understanding and uniform application of the rules. GM documents do not impose obligations but help interpret regulatory intent.

## How to use it

For exam preparation, the Easy Access Rules document is the most convenient reference because:
- Regulations and guidance appear side by side
- Article references (e.g. UAS.OPEN.060) are used directly in exam questions
- Free to download from the EASA document library

## Official source

Always download the latest version from the EASA document library — the document is updated as amendments are adopted.
