Latvia has designated UAS Geographical Zones (UGZ) under Regulation (EU) 2019/947. These are published in the BGKIS system and define where drone operations are permitted, restricted, or prohibited without prior authorisation.

## How to check zones

Before any flight, check the BGKIS interactive map:
- **airspace.lv/drones** — quick public overview
- **ais.lgs.lv/page/UAS_geozones** — official Latvia AIS UGZ map with current activations

Both show colour-coded zones. Always check both the zone type and any active temporary restrictions (NOTAMs).

## Zone types in Latvia

| Zone type | Meaning |
|-----------|---------|
| Green | Operations permitted without prior notification |
| Yellow | Operations permitted with prior notification to BGKIS or CAA |
| Red | Prohibited without specific CAA authorisation |
| Riga CTR | Control zone — coordination with Riga ATC mandatory |

## BGKIS flight filing

For flights in notification-required zones, file a flight notification via the BGKIS portal. The portal is available in Latvian and confirms receipt electronically.

## Official source

The authoritative zone boundaries and procedures are maintained by CAA Latvia and published through the official BGKIS portal and AIS Latvia.
