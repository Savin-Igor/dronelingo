UAS operators in Latvia must register with CAA Latvia through the e.caa.gov.lv portal before conducting any operations requiring registration (subcategories A1 with C1 class or above, A2, and A3).

## Who must register

Registration is required for any natural or legal person who operates a UA:
- of C1 class or above in A1 subcategory,
- in A2 subcategory,
- in A3 subcategory,
- or any UA above 250 g regardless of class.

## Registration process

1. Create an account on **e.caa.gov.lv**.
2. Submit the registration application with personal or company details.
3. Receive the operator registration number (format: LVA-OP-XXXXXXX or similar).
4. Affix the registration number to every UA you operate.
5. Maintain third-party liability insurance (mandatory).

## Registration number marking

The operator registration number must be permanently and legibly marked on the UA. It may also be encoded in a QR code affixed to the UA.

## Official source

The registration portal and current requirements are at e.caa.gov.lv.
