# Privacy policy

dronelingo.eu operates in Latvia and complies with the General Data
Protection Regulation (GDPR, EU 2016/679) and Latvia's Personal Data
Processing Law.

## What we process

| Data type | When | Why |
|-----------|------|-----|
| Browser storage (`localStorage`) | During practice and exam runs | So your progress and answers stay on your device |
| Email (optional) | At Pass Guarantee checkout | To send a thank-you note |
| Technical logs | Server requests | Security + error tracing |

## What we **don't** do

- We do not sell data to third parties.
- We do not run cross-site advertising pixels.
- We do not store your practice or exam runs server-side until you
  sign in.

## Retention

- Browser-local data stays on your device until you clear it.
- Payment-related email — up to 5 years (legal requirement).
- Technical logs — up to 90 days.

## Your rights

Under GDPR you can request:

- access to your data;
- correction or erasure;
- data portability.

Contact: `privacy@dronelingo.eu`.

## Supervisor

Latvia's Data State Inspectorate
([dvi.gov.lv](https://dvi.gov.lv)).
