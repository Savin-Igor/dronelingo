# Registration guide: from `0` to your A1/A3 certificate

This guide walks through every one of the 8 steps Latvian
**Civil Aviation Agency (CAA Latvia)** requires for Open-category A1/A3
drones. Each step is official, free (except the €5/year operator
registration), and all happen on a single portal:
[e.caa.gov.lv](https://e.caa.gov.lv/).

> **Source:** Public CAA Latvia drone guidance at
> [droni.caa.gov.lv](https://droni.caa.gov.lv/), last reviewed on
> 2026-05-08; Reg (EU) 2019/947 Art. 14; Cabinet Regulation No. 457.

---

## Step 1 — Understand the two roles

Latvia recognises two intertwined roles:

- **UAS operator** — natural or legal person under whose name the
  flights are performed.
- **Remote pilot** — natural person who flies the UAS.

A private pilot flying in their own name **must register as both**.
Companies register the company as operator and each employee as a
remote pilot.

---

## Step 2 — Register as a remote pilot (FREE)

1. Open [e.caa.gov.lv](https://e.caa.gov.lv/) and sign in via
   `Latvija.lv` (Smart-ID, eParaksts, or bank ID).
2. Choose "Register a remote pilot".
3. Confirm personal data and accept the terms.
4. Receive your unique number: `LVA-RP-############`.

> **You need this number for the exam and to bind the pilot to the
> operator.** It is not used to mark the drone.

---

## Step 3 — Register as a UAS operator (€5 / year)

Operator registration is mandatory if the drone:

- has ≥ 250 g mass **or**
- can transfer > 80 J of kinetic energy on impact **or**
- carries a photo/video camera (excluding toys per Directive
  2009/48/EC) **or**
- flies in the Specific category.

1. [e.caa.gov.lv](https://e.caa.gov.lv/) → "Register a UAS operator".
2. Pay €5 (bank transfer).
3. Receive the `LVA############` operator number.

> Done **once**, regardless of how many drones you own. Valid for 1
> year — renew annually.

---

## Step 4 — Mark each drone with the operator number

Every drone you operate must carry your **readable** operator number
(`LVA############`).

- Preferably on the body, in plain sight.
- The battery compartment is acceptable if the body is too small.
- Must be readable **without tools** (no camera, no magnifier).

## Step 7 — Download the certificate

After a successful pass [e.caa.gov.lv](https://e.caa.gov.lv/) issues your **A1/A3 knowledge
certificate PDF**. The certificate:

- lives in the CAA UAS information system;
- is **valid for 5 years**;
- is **recognised across the EU**.

## Step 8 — Before your first flight

Registration and the exam are necessary but not sufficient. Before
every flight:

1. **Check UGZ** at [`airspace.lv/drones`](https://airspace.lv/drones).
2. If in a restricted zone — **submit a BGKIS application**
   (mandatory since 2025-01-01).
3. **Liability insurance** — A1 (C1), A2, A3 up to 20 kg ⇒ minimum
   **€50 000** (Cabinet Reg. No. 447).
4. **Mark** the drone as yours and respect VLOS, the 120 m cap, and
   distance from uninvolved persons.
