# Regulatory Framework

This page consolidates the applicable EU and Latvian legislation governing unmanned aircraft (drone) operations.

## EU-level regulations {#eu-regulations}

| Act | Description |
|-----|-------------|
| [Regulation (EU) 2018/1139](https://eur-lex.europa.eu/eli/reg/2018/1139/oj/?locale=EN) | Common rules in the field of civil aviation (incl. UAS framework) |
| [Regulation (EU) 2019/947](https://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/EN/TXT/?uri=CELEX%3A02019R0947-20200606) | Rules and procedures for the operation of unmanned aircraft |
| [Regulation (EU) 2019/945](https://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/EN/TXT/?uri=CELEX%3A02019R0945-20200809) | Technical requirements for UAS and third-country UAS operators |
| [EASA AMC/GM to Reg 2019/947](https://www.easa.europa.eu/en/document-library/acceptable-means-of-compliance-and-guidance-materials/reg/unmanned-aircraft-systems-uas) | Accepted means of compliance and guidance for Reg (EU) 2019/947 |
| [EASA Easy Access Rules — UAS](https://www.easa.europa.eu/document-library/easy-access-rules/easy-access-rules-unmanned-aircraft-systems-regulation-eu) | Consolidated overview of Reg 2019/945 + 2019/947 + AMC/GM |

> National regulations for other EU Member States: [easa.europa.eu/domains/civil-drones/naa](https://www.easa.europa.eu/domains/civil-drones/naa)

## Other applicable rules {#other-rules}

- **Privacy and personal data protection** ([State Data Inspectorate](https://www.dvi.gov.lv/)): applies to personal data collected during aerial surveillance.
- **Photography/filming of critical infrastructure**: prohibited without authorisation if the site carries the appropriate notice ([Latvian legislation](https://likumi.lv/ta/id/324689)).
- **Use of radio frequencies** ([VASES](https://www.vases.lv/lv/content/rpas-droni)): some bands permitted without a licence; maritime/aeronautical emergency frequencies are prohibited.
