# Regulations and official sources

This page lists the official regulations and CAA Latvia pages that dronelingo cites across lessons, practice questions, exam review, and blog posts.

## EU primary law

### Regulation (EU) 2019/947

Official EUR-Lex text for UAS operating rules and procedures in the Open and Specific categories.

- [EUR-Lex: Regulation (EU) 2019/947](https://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/EN/TXT/?uri=CELEX%3A32019R0947)

### Regulation (EU) 2019/945

Official EUR-Lex text for drone class requirements, remote ID, geo-awareness, and manufacturer obligations.

- [EUR-Lex: Regulation (EU) 2019/945](https://eur-lex.europa.eu/eli/reg_del/2019/945/oj/eng)

### GDPR

Official EUR-Lex text for Regulation (EU) 2016/679 on personal data protection.

- [EUR-Lex: Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (GDPR)](https://eur-lex.europa.eu/eli/reg/2016/679/oj/eng)

## EASA guidance

### Easy Access Rules for UAS

Consolidated EASA guidance for Regulations (EU) 2019/947 and 2019/945, including AMC and GM.

- [EASA: Easy Access Rules for Unmanned Aircraft Systems](https://www.easa.europa.eu/en/document-library/easy-access-rules/easy-access-rules-unmanned-aircraft-systems-regulations-eu)
