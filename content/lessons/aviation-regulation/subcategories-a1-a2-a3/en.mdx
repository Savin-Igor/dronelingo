# A1, A2 and A3 subcategories in detail

Open category splits into three subcategories — by UAS class and
distance to people.

## A1 — over people

| Class | Pilot certificate | Note |
|-------|-------------------|------|
| C0 (< 250 g) | **None required** | Overflight of individuals allowed |
| C1 | A1/A3 online exam | No flight over uninvolved persons |

## A2 — close to people (5 m / 30 m)

| Class | Pilot certificate |
|-------|-------------------|
| C2 | **A1/A3 + extra A2 exam** |

The A2 add-on exam requires **practical self-study** plus a 60-question
theory test, ≥75 %, at a recognised organisation.

## A3 — far from people

| Class | Pilot certificate |
|-------|-------------------|
| C2 / C3 / C4 | A1/A3 online exam |
| Legacy (< 25 kg) | A1/A3 online exam |

A3 requires **150 m from residential areas** and **30 m from
uninvolved persons** or 1:1 (≥ flight altitude).

## Universal limits

All three subcategories share:

- max **120 m** above ground;
- VLOS;
- no flight over assemblies of people.

> **Source:** Reg (EU) 2019/947 UAS.OPEN.020 / 030 / 040; EASA Easy
> Access Rules for UAS — Subcategories A1 / A2 / A3.
