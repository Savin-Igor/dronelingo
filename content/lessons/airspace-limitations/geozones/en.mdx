# UAS geographical zones in Latvia

Before every flight the pilot **must check UAS geographical zones (UGZ)**.
In Latvia the only legally binding source is
[airspace.lv/drones](https://airspace.lv/drones).

## Three UGZ types

- **Information** — no restriction, just notification (e.g., bird reserve).
- **Restricted** — allowed under conditions: BGKIS application, altitude
  cap, or special authorisation.
- **Prohibited** — flights are not allowed (airport vicinity, military
  sites).

## BGKIS applications

Since 1 January 2025 **flights in restricted zones require an application
in the BGKIS system**. Without an approved application the flight is
illegal regardless of UAS class.

## What a UGZ authorisation does **not** grant

A UGZ authorisation increases access — but **does not expand the limits
of the Open category**. The following still apply:

- 120 m maximum height above ground;
- VLOS requirement;
- ban on flying over assemblies of people.

## Border zones

Since 2025 the entire Latvia–Russia and Latvia–Belarus border is **EVR17
Border East** — closed for Open category flights.

> **Source:** Reg (EU) 2019/947 Art. 15; `airspace.lv/drones` (CAA Latvia),
