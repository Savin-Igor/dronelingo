# Optional insurance covers

Mandatory third-party liability covers **damage to others**. Full
professional protection needs additional policies.

## Hull (the UAS itself) insurance

Covers damage to the UAS:

- **Crash** (pilot error, fly-away, lost link);
- **Theft** or loss;
- **Water damage** if the manufacturer drops its IPxx claim.

Typically 5–10 % of UAS value per year, with a **deductible** of
€200–500.

## Professional liability

For commercial operators (photo/video, inspection, security). Covers:

- Client damages if the work fails the contract.
- Data loss (footage, GIS data).
- IP / copyright claims on materials used.

## Payload insurance

Cameras, sensors, lidar modules separately. Often more expensive than
the UAS, so cover is priced separately.

## Export / transport policy

For travel jobs in other EU countries. The base Latvian policy is
**valid across the EU**, but extra cover is sensible in some
jurisdictions (e.g. Germany imposes higher demands on commercial
operators).

## How to choose

| Scenario | Minimum stack |
|----------|---------------|
| Recreational < 250 g | Only mandatory (or none required) |
| Recreational C1 / C2 / C3 | Mandatory + hull |
| Commercial photo / video | Mandatory + hull + professional |
| Travel jobs in other EU countries | + transport policy |

> **Source:** Reg (EC) 785/2004; commercial insurer product offerings
> (BTA, Balta, Gjensidige); CAA Latvia guidance.
