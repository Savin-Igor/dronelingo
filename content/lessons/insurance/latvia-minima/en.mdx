# Latvia mandatory insurance minima

In Latvia a UAS operator is **required to hold third-party liability
insurance** — regardless of whether the flight is commercial or
recreational. The minima are set by Cabinet Regulation No. 447.

## Cover amounts

| Class / mass | Minimum cover |
|--------------|----------------|
| C0 (< 250 g) | **Not required** |
| A1 (C1), A2, A3 (up to 20 kg) | **€50 000** |
| A3 (20–25 kg, C3 / C4) | **750 000 SDR** |
| Specific category (< 500 kg) | **750 000 SDR** |

## What the minimum covers

Third-party **bodily injury and property damage** caused during a UAS
flight. Damage to the UAS itself (hull) **is not included**.

## What SDR means

SDR = Special Drawing Rights — IMF currency basket. 750 000 SDR ≈
€900 000 (rate fluctuates).

## Proof

The policy must be shown on request to a CAA Latvia inspector. Without
a policy the operator is **not qualified to fly** and receives an
administrative fine.

## Optional cover

- **Hull insurance** — covers the UAS itself (crash, theft).
- **Employer's liability** — for commercial operators.
- **Payload insurance** — cameras, sensor equipment.

> **Source:** Cabinet Reg. No. 447 (10.07.2018); Reg (EC) 785/2004 on
> insurance requirements for air carriers.
