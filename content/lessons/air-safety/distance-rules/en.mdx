# Distance from uninvolved persons

Open-category limits depend on the subcategory and UAS class.

## A1 — over people (with caveats)

| Class | Rule |
|-------|------|
| **C0** (< 250 g) | Overflight of individuals allowed; **never** over assemblies |
| **C1** | "Reasonably expected not to fly over uninvolved persons" |

C1 does not fly directly over people; if someone enters the path,
abort or divert.

## A2 — close to people (5 m / 30 m)

| Mode | Minimum horizontal distance |
|------|------------------------------|
| C2 in low-speed (≤ 3 m/s) | **5 m** |
| C2 normal | **30 m** |

A2 requires a **competence certificate** (extra exam after A1/A3).

## A3 — far from people (1:1 rule)

- ≥ **30 m** from uninvolved persons or **1:1** (≥ flight altitude).
- ≥ **150 m** from residential / commercial / industrial areas.
- ≥ a "2-second flight distance" at max speed.

## Assemblies

**Overflight of assemblies of people is forbidden in every Open
subcategory** regardless of UAS class.

> **Source:** Reg (EU) 2019/947 UAS.OPEN.020/030/040; EASA Easy Access
> Rules for UAS — Subcategories A1 / A2 / A3.
