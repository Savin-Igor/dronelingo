## Editorial constraints

- No build guides, no weaponization how-to, no evasion guidance.
- “Cannot be jammed” may only refer to the RF command-and-video link in a fiber-optic architecture; the article must still explain platform limits and new vulnerabilities.
- Procurement-style “best” article must be explicit about use cases: portable SAR, all-weather enterprise, heavy thermal, docked autonomy, and software ecosystem fit.
- Military examples must be described analytically, not tactically.
