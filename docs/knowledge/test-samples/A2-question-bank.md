# A2 — Sample Question Bank

30 EASA-aligned A2 questions captured from a real A2 CofC mock exam (Skyviation UK). UK-flavoured but rules are the same as in Latvia (Reg 2019/947).

> ⚠ **Без официальных ответов** — ниже даны рекомендуемые ответы по EASA AMC/GM. Используйте для самопроверки. Не копировать в публичные/коммерческие материалы.

Latvia A2 official exam: 30 questions / 30 min / ≥75 % to pass / темы: meteorology, flight performance, ground risk mitigation. Languages: LV / EN.

## METEOROLOĢIJA

### Q1. Why should you avoid a Cb (cumulonimbus)?
- A. Car radio interference
- B. ✅ **Danger of lightning strike and electrostatic interference**
- C. Get wet
- D. Loud noises

### Q2. Which is NOT a form of turbulence?
- A. Thermal
- B. Shear
- C. Mechanical
- D. ✅ **Vortex** *(vortex is a flow phenomenon, not a turbulence category)*

### Q3. Isobars are lines of equal what?
- A. Height
- B. Front
- C. ✅ **Pressure**
- D. Degrees

### Q4. What happens if isobars are close together?
- A. ✅ **High winds**
- B. Cold temperature
- C. Warm front approaching
- D. Low winds

### Q5. With high humidity and negative wind-chill, what could you expect to form on your propellers?
- A. Water
- B. ✅ **Ice**
- C. Clouds
- D. Dust

## UAS LIDOJUMA VEIKTSPĒJA

### Q6. What is MTOM?
- A. Maximum take-off Meter
- B. Minimum take-off Mass
- C. Mean take-off Mass
- D. ✅ **Maximum take-off Mass**

### Q7. C-rating on batteries stands for what?
- A. ✅ **Current (discharge rate)**
- B. Coulomb
- C. Charge
- D. Crystal

### Q8. At what battery temperature should you NOT attempt to charge?
- A. 22 °C
- B. 25 °C
- C. 23 °C
- D. ✅ **Over 45 °C**

### Q9. What does IP stand for?
- A. Internal Pressure
- B. ✅ **Ingress Protection**
- C. Initial Process
- D. Internal Problem

### Q10. What conditions are best for generating lift?
- A. Hot summer's day
- B. ✅ **Cold winter's morning** *(denser air → more lift, lower density altitude)*
- C. Midday during a thunderstorm
- D. Top of a mountain

## ZEMES RISKU MAZINĀŠANA

### Q11. What is the max height you can fly from the surface (open category)?
- A. 300 ft
- B. ✅ **400 ft / 120 m AGL** *(EU = 120 m)*
- C. 150 m
- D. 150 ft

### Q12. Using the 1:1 rule, what is the minimum distance from uninvolved persons if you are 40 m high?
- A. ✅ **40 m**
- B. 30 m
- C. 5 m
- D. 25 m

### Q13. What speed is your aircraft limited to in low-speed mode (A2 close-mode)?
- A. 60 m/s
- B. 30 m/s
- C. ✅ **3 m/s**
- D. 3 mph

### Q14. What distance must you keep from a group of uninvolved people in the A2 transitional period?
- A. 150 ft
- B. ✅ **150 m** *(transitional only — without C2 marking)*
- C. 30 m
- D. 50 m

> Без C2 — 150 м (как в A3). С C2 — 30 м (или меньше с low-speed + 1:1).
