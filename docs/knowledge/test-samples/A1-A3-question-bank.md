# A1/A3 — Sample Question Bank

Sample questions modelled on the EASA A1/A3 syllabus. Use to practice; **not** the official CAA Latvia bank.
Sources: easy-quizzz.com (free demo PDF), Megadron blog, EASA AMC/GM Reg 2019/947.

Format: each question shows the answer + a short rationale.

## TEMA 1 — Aviācijas regulējums (Air Law)

### Q1. Under EASA Open Category rules, who is ultimately responsible for the safe conduct of a drone flight?
- A. The pilot and the commissioning client share equal responsibility
- B. ✅ **Only the pilot and/or his/her organisation (operator)**
- C. The client commissioning the work assumes full responsibility

> Responsibility rests solely with the remote pilot and/or the UAS operator. (UAS.OPEN.060)

### Q2. As a remote pilot, are you always obliged to comply with the user manual?
- A. Yes, always
- B. No
- C. ✅ **Yes, but only if the manual does not conflict with legal/regulatory provisions**

> Manufacturer instructions are mandatory unless they conflict with regulation; regulation prevails.

### Q3. To register on the e-services portal (`e.caa.gov.lv`), is a pilot certificate required first?
- A. Yes, in any case
- B. ✅ **No — UAS operator registration does not require a pilot certificate**
- C. No, but it must be completed before the first flight

> The UAS *operator* registers; the *remote pilot* qualification is separate.

### Q4. To fly an A1/A3 sub-category, what is the minimum age of the remote pilot in Latvia?
- A. 14
- B. ✅ **16**
- C. 18

> Minimum age 16 (Reg 2019/947). Exceptions: < 250 g toy, supervised by qualified pilot, model-aircraft club.

## TEMA 2 — Gaisa telpas ierobežojumi (Airspace Limitations)

### Q5. What is the maximum allowed flight altitude for UAS in the open category?
- A. ✅ **120 m above ground or water surface**
- B. 150 m
- C. 400 ft / 122 m AGL

> Hard limit 120 m; UAS geographical zones may impose lower limits.

### Q6. In a UAS geographical zone where flights require prior approval, what must be done before flying?
- A. ✅ **Submit a flight application to the zone manager via BGKIS / e.caa.gov.lv**
- B. Just stay below 50 m
- C. Notify the airport tower

> Mandatory since 2025-01-01 (MK Nr. 248 §53, §73).

## TEMA 3 — Cilvēka veiktspējas ierobežojumi (Human Performance)

### Q7. What conditions are best for safely generating lift and stable flight?
- A. Hot summer day with strong gusts
- B. ✅ **Cold winter morning with stable air**
- C. Top of a mountain at midday in a thunderstorm
- D. Strong tailwind

> Cold, dense, stable air provides best lift and predictable handling.

### Q8. Why must FPV (First-Person View) flights have an observer next to the pilot?
- A. To carry batteries
- B. ✅ **To maintain VLOS situational awareness around the UAS**
- C. To replay video later

> The pilot wearing FPV goggles cannot see surrounding airspace; observer ensures separation.

## TEMA 4 — Ekspluatācijas procedūras (Operational Procedures)

### Q9. What is the most important emergency procedure if the radio link to the UAS is lost?
- A. ✅ **The UAS should automatically execute Return-to-Home (RTH) or land safely per the programmed fail-safe**
- B. Switch off your transmitter
- C. Wait 5 minutes

> All Open-category UAS must have a fail-safe; pilot must understand its behaviour pre-flight.

### Q10. In Open A1, when FOLLOW-ME mode is active, what is the maximum distance from the pilot?
- A. ✅ **50 m**
- B. 100 m
- C. 150 m

> AMC1 to UAS.OPEN.060 — FOLLOW-ME is limited to 50 m so pilot can intervene.

### Q11. Are flights over assemblies of people allowed in the open category?
- A. Yes, with C0 marking
- B. ✅ **No — never in any open subcategory**
- C. Yes, if announced in advance

> Assemblies of people are forbidden in the entire open category. Article UAS.OPEN.020/030/040.

### Q12. The "1:1 rule" in A3 means…
- A. One UAS, one pilot
- B. ✅ **Horizontal distance to uninvolved persons must be at least equal to flight altitude (and ≥ 30 m)**
- C. 1 metre per kilogram

## TEMA 5 — Vispārīgas zināšanas par UAS (UAS General Knowledge)

### Q13. What does MTOM mean?
- A. Maximum take-off meter
- B. Minimum take-off mass
- C. ✅ **Maximum Take-Off Mass**

### Q14. The C-class identification marking is applied by…
- A. The operator after measuring the drone
- B. ✅ **The manufacturer, declaring conformity to Reg (EU) 2019/945**
- C. CAA Latvia upon registration

### Q15. CE marking on a drone is the same as a C-class marking?
- A. Yes
- B. ✅ **No — CE attests general product safety; C-class is specific to UAS technical requirements**

## TEMA 6 — Privātums un personas datu aizsardzība (Privacy)

### Q16. A drone equipped with a camera is flown for personal use over a public park. What rules apply?
- A. None — it's recreational
- B. ✅ **GDPR + national privacy law: avoid recording identifiable people without consent; the operator must register**
- C. Only commercial use is regulated

> Datu valsts inspekcija (Latvian DPA) provides guidance.

## TEMA 7 — Apdrošināšana (Insurance)

### Q17. In Latvia, mandatory civil-liability insurance for an A2 (C2) drone has minimum cover of…
- A. 25 000 EUR
- B. ✅ **50 000 EUR**
- C. 750 000 SDR

> MK Nr. 447 — A2/C2 minimum 50 000 EUR; > 20 kg or specific category → 750 000 SDR.

### Q18. Is general civil-liability insurance mandatory across the EU for all UAS?
- A. ✅ **No — for < 20 kg it is set by national law of each Member State**
- B. Yes, EU-wide
- C. Only for commercial flights

## TEMA 8 — Drošība (Security)

### Q19. If you injure a person with your drone, you must…
- A. Wait until your next flight
- B. ✅ **Submit an occurrence report (≤ 72 h) via `e.caa.gov.lv/incidents` or aviationreporting.eu**
- C. Inform only the city council

### Q20. What weight requires UAS operator registration regardless of camera?
- A. < 250 g
- B. ✅ **≥ 250 g (or any weight if camera/sensor present, or specific category)**
- C. ≥ 1 kg

## TEMA 9 — Meteoroloģija un lidojuma veiktspēja (Meteorology & Flight Performance)

### Q21. The sky-coverage abbreviation BKN ("broken") corresponds to what cloud cover in eighths (oktas)?
- A. 1–2 oktas
- B. 3–4 oktas
- C. ✅ **5–7 oktas**

### Q22. With high humidity and a negative wind-chill, what may form on your propellers?
- A. Water
- B. ✅ **Ice**
- C. Dust

### Q23. Isobars represent lines of equal…
- A. Altitude
- B. Front
- C. ✅ **Atmospheric pressure**

### Q24. Closely-spaced isobars indicate…
- A. ✅ **Strong winds**
- B. Calm conditions
- C. Cold front passage

### Q25. Why should you avoid a cumulonimbus (Cb) cloud?
- A. Loud noises
- B. ✅ **Lightning, severe turbulence, hail, electrostatic interference**
- C. Just to keep the drone dry

### Q26. At what battery temperature should you NOT attempt to charge?
- A. 22 °C
- B. 25 °C
- C. ✅ **Above 45 °C (or below 0 °C, depending on chemistry)**

> Always follow manufacturer data; exceeding limits causes thermal runaway.
